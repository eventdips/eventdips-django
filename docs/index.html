A platform to connect teachers to students for competitions

About EvENTDIPS

Its an open platform to bridge the gap between the students and teachers in the process of selecting teams for various competitions.

The hassle the teacher has to go through for selecting the team is quite a lot and keeps increasing with the number of students looking forward to go.

We have tried to bridge that gap between the students and teachers by taking the whole process online and giving the students a quick and easy way to apply for a competition and the teacher an efficient way to select the students while looking over their portfolios and previous competitions.
